Meet the Team

  • Akshay Saxena
    Advisor, Life Coach, Gandalf

    Akshay is the Co-Founder of Avanti Fellows and an Ashoka fellow. He was awarded the Draper Richards Kaplan Fellowship and Echoing Green Fellowship in 2012 for his work at Avanti. Akshay graduated with a bachelor’s degree in chemical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay and is currently on a leave of absence from the MBA program at the Harvard Business School.

  • Aman Dalmia
    Co-founder, AI Wizard, Hobbit

    Aman is a Co-Founder of Plio and leads AI strategy and implementation. Before starting his venture, he worked as a Research Fellow at Wadhwani AI, working on using AI for Social Good. For the past few months, he has been one of the lead researchers in the Cough Against COVID effort which has demonstrated the utility of cough sounds alone as a biomarker for COVID-19 with statistical significance. Aman graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati.

  • Deepansh Mathur
    Co-founder, Frontend Ninja, Hobbit

    Deepansh is a Co-Founder of Plio and leads the Design and Development front at Plio. He’s currently working at Fair Issac Corporation (FICO) as a Solution Integration Consultant and is contributing 80% of his efforts to Plio. He has always been passionate about using technology for social good and building solutions that are free to use and impactful. Deepansh graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Biotechnology from the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati.

  • Joanna Sundaram
    Co-founder, Data Guru, Elf

    Joanna is responsible for directing and implementing Avanti Fellow’s research and policy initiatives, and helps with focusing the data analysis at Plio. She has six years of professional experience across India, the US, and Sub-Saharan Africa. Prior to joining Avanti Fellows, she worked at the World Bank in Washington DC. Joanna started her career as a Teach for India fellow in 2013 has worked at Sunshine Schools in New Delhi and Gray Matters Capital in Bengaluru.

  • Vandana Goyal
    Advisor, Gandalf

    Vandana is the Executive Trustee at Avanti Fellows and leads all operations and fundraising strategy at Avanti Fellows. In addition to her work at Avanti, Vandana is a Board Member and former Chief Executive Officer of the Akanksha Foundation. Vandana has graduated from Claremont McKenna College with a degree in Economics.
